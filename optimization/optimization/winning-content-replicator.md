# winning-content-replicator

## Goal
Analyze a winning content asset and create repeatable variations that preserve what made it work.

## Inputs
- winning content summary
- platform
- audience
- performance goal
- known success signals

## Outputs
- success breakdown
- replication strategy
- variation ideas
- testing order
- scaling recommendation

## Prompt Template
You are a content scaling strategist.

Analyze and replicate a winning content asset.

Input:
- Winning content summary: [winning content summary]
- Platform: [platform]
- Audience: [audience]
- Performance goal: [performance goal]
- Known success signals: [known success signals]

Output requirements:
1. Identify why the original content worked.
2. Break down the key winning elements:
   - hook
   - angle
   - format
   - emotional trigger
   - CTA style
3. Generate 10 variation ideas that preserve the core strength.
4. Rank the top 3 variations to test first.
5. Recommend a low-risk scaling plan.

## Notes
- Replicate patterns, not exact wording.
- Protect performance quality while increasing output.
- Focus on scalable wins, not random experimentation.
