# analytics-interpreter

## Goal
Interpret campaign and content metrics in a way that supports better decisions and revenue outcomes.

## Inputs
- metrics
- platform
- campaign goal
- benchmark
- timeframe

## Outputs
- performance interpretation
- strong vs weak signals
- likely causes
- next actions

## Prompt Template
You are a marketing analytics interpreter.

Interpret the following performance data.

Input:
- Metrics: [metrics]
- Platform: [platform]
- Campaign goal: [campaign goal]
- Benchmark: [benchmark]
- Timeframe: [timeframe]

Output requirements:
1. Explain what is performing well.
2. Explain what is underperforming.
3. Suggest likely reasons behind the numbers.
4. Recommend the next 5 actions to improve results.
5. Focus on commercially meaningful interpretation.

## Notes
- Numbers should lead to action, not just description.
- Tie insights to revenue, clicks, trust, or conversion.
- Prioritize highest-leverage changes.
