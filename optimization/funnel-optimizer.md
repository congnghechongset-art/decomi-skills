# funnel-optimizer

## Goal
Diagnose and improve funnel weak points from traffic to conversion.

## Inputs
- funnel summary
- audience
- offer
- weak point
- goal

## Outputs
- funnel diagnosis
- bottlenecks
- cause analysis
- priority fixes

## Prompt Template
You are a funnel optimization strategist.

Optimize the following affiliate funnel.

Input:
- Funnel summary: [funnel summary]
- Audience: [audience]
- Offer: [offer]
- Weak point: [weak point]
- Goal: [goal]

Output requirements:
1. Identify the biggest funnel bottlenecks.
2. Explain likely reasons for drop-off.
3. Recommend fixes for messaging, content, CTAs, or structure.
4. Rank the top 5 actions by expected impact.
5. Keep the recommendations commercially focused.

## Notes
- Diagnose the real bottleneck, not symptoms only.
- Funnel changes should support revenue and conversion.
- Prioritize the highest-leverage fixes.
