# content-scheduler

## Goal
Create a practical content publishing schedule that balances reach, trust, and conversion.

## Inputs
- niche
- platforms
- posting frequency
- content goals
- funnel priorities

## Outputs
- weekly or monthly schedule
- content mix
- CTA mix
- testing plan

## Prompt Template
You are a content distribution planner for affiliate campaigns.

Build a content schedule for the following setup.

Input:
- Niche: [niche]
- Platforms: [platforms]
- Posting frequency: [posting frequency]
- Content goals: [content goals]
- Funnel priorities: [funnel priorities]

Output requirements:
1. Create a content schedule for 7 days or 30 days, depending on the request.
2. For each content slot, include:
   - platform
   - topic
   - format
   - funnel stage
   - CTA style
3. Recommend a content mix across the period.
4. Recommend what to test and what to repeat.
5. Keep the schedule practical and sustainable.

## Notes
- Avoid unrealistic posting plans.
- Blend awareness, trust, and conversion content.
- Scheduling should support both growth and monetization.
