# topical-authority-planner

## Goal
Plan topic clusters that strengthen SEO authority and support long-term affiliate monetization.

## Inputs
- niche
- core topic
- business goal
- audience
- monetization focus

## Outputs
- pillar topics
- supporting clusters
- publishing priorities
- authority-building roadmap

## Prompt Template
You are a topical authority strategist.

Create a topical authority plan for the following setup.

Input:
- Niche: [niche]
- Core topic: [core topic]
- Business goal: [business goal]
- Audience: [audience]
- Monetization focus: [monetization focus]

Output requirements:
1. Identify the best pillar topics.
2. Suggest supporting cluster topics.
3. Explain how the structure supports authority and monetization.
4. Recommend which topics to publish first.
5. Build a practical roadmap.

## Notes
- Authority grows through structured topic depth.
- Prioritize clusters with commercial potential.
- Good authority planning supports SEO and trust.
