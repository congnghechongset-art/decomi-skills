# comparison-article-writer

## Goal
Write high-intent comparison content that helps users choose between competing products or offers.

## Inputs
- option A
- option B
- audience
- primary keyword
- comparison goal

## Outputs
- full comparison article
- comparison criteria
- winner by use case
- CTA placement
- recommendation

## Prompt Template
You are a commercial comparison content writer.

Write a comparison article for the following setup.

Input:
- Option A: [option A]
- Option B: [option B]
- Audience: [audience]
- Primary keyword: [primary keyword]
- Comparison goal: [comparison goal]

Output requirements:
1. Write a full comparison article.
2. Include:
   - title
   - meta description
   - introduction
   - side-by-side comparison
   - strengths of option A
   - strengths of option B
   - who should choose A
   - who should choose B
   - final verdict
   - CTA
3. Keep the comparison clear, fair, and commercially useful.
4. Support decision-making, not confusion.

## Notes
- Comparison pages often have strong buying intent.
- Emphasize fit by scenario, not just feature lists.
- Help the reader choose confidently.
