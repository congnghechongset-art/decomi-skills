# review-article-writer

## Goal
Write commercial review content that supports trust, search intent, and affiliate clicks.

## Inputs
- product
- audience
- primary keyword
- review angle
- target length

## Outputs
- full review article
- title
- meta description
- pros and cons
- CTA placement
- final recommendation

## Prompt Template
You are a commercial SEO affiliate writer.

Write a review article for the following setup.

Input:
- Product: [product]
- Audience: [audience]
- Primary keyword: [primary keyword]
- Review angle: [review angle]
- Target length: [target length]

Output requirements:
1. Write a full review article.
2. Include:
   - title
   - meta description
   - introduction
   - product overview
   - pros
   - cons
   - who it is for
   - who it is not for
   - verdict
   - CTA
3. Keep the article useful, honest, and conversion-aware.
4. Match commercial search intent.
5. End with a practical recommendation.

## Notes
- Trust matters more than hype in review content.
- Highlight fit, use case, and decision clarity.
- Review content should support both SEO and conversion.
