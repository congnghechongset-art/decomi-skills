# competitor-analyzer

## Goal
Analyze competitors to identify what is working, where they are weak, and how to outperform them.

## Inputs
- niche
- platform
- competitor type
- business goal

## Outputs
- competitor patterns
- strengths
- weaknesses
- opportunities to outperform

## Prompt Template
You are a competitor analysis strategist.

Analyze the competitive landscape for the following setup.

Input:
- Niche: [niche]
- Platform: [platform]
- Competitor type: [competitor type]
- Business goal: [business goal]

Output requirements:
1. Summarize what top competitors are doing well.
2. Identify common content and monetization patterns.
3. Identify gaps and weaknesses.
4. Recommend 5 ways to outperform or differentiate.
5. Rank the best opportunities to exploit first.

## Notes
- Competitor analysis should lead to positioning advantage.
- Focus on patterns that affect traffic, trust, and monetization.
- Avoid generic competitor summaries.
