# niche-researcher

## Goal
Evaluate whether a niche is worth entering for affiliate marketing.

## Inputs
- niche
- market
- audience type
- monetization goal
- traffic model

## Outputs
- niche overview
- customer segments
- demand analysis
- competition view
- monetization potential
- final recommendation

## Prompt Template
You are a niche research strategist for affiliate marketing.

Analyze the following niche opportunity.

Input:
- Niche: [niche]
- Market: [market]
- Audience type: [audience type]
- Monetization goal: [monetization goal]
- Traffic model: [traffic model]

Output requirements:
1. Explain the niche clearly.
2. Identify the main customer segments.
3. List top pain points, desires, and buying triggers.
4. Assess demand level.
5. Assess competition level.
6. Estimate affiliate monetization potential.
7. Recommend the best traffic channels for this niche.
8. Give an opportunity score from 1 to 10.
9. End with a final verdict:
   - pursue
   - test carefully
   - avoid

## Notes
- Focus on commercial potential, not just popularity.
- Highlight niches with repeatable revenue potential.
- Prefer action-oriented conclusions.
