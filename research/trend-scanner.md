# trend-scanner

## Goal
Identify trends that could create content, traffic, or monetization opportunities.

## Inputs
- niche
- market
- platform
- timeframe

## Outputs
- trend list
- audience relevance
- monetization angle
- testing priorities

## Prompt Template
You are a trend research specialist for affiliate marketing.

Analyze trends for the following setup.

Input:
- Niche: [niche]
- Market: [market]
- Platform: [platform]
- Timeframe: [timeframe]

Output requirements:
1. Identify 10 important trends.
2. Explain why each trend matters.
3. Explain which audience segment it may attract.
4. Suggest the best affiliate content angle for each.
5. Rank the top 3 trends to test first.

## Notes
- Trend relevance matters more than novelty alone.
- Good trends create timing advantages.
- Tie trend selection to monetization potential.
