# affiliate-network-explorer

## Goal
Compare affiliate networks and identify which ones are most suitable for a given niche and operator level.

## Inputs
- niche
- market
- product type
- experience level

## Outputs
- network comparison
- strengths and weaknesses
- fit ranking
- recommended starting networks

## Prompt Template
You are an affiliate network strategist.

Compare affiliate networks for the following setup.

Input:
- Niche: [niche]
- Market: [market]
- Product type: [product type]
- Experience level: [experience level]

Output requirements:
1. Compare 8 affiliate networks.
2. Include:
   - network type
   - ease of approval
   - offer quality
   - payout appeal
   - beginner friendliness
3. Rank the best options.
4. Recommend the top 3 networks to start with.
5. Explain why they fit this setup.

## Notes
- Network choice affects offer access and speed of execution.
- Choose based on fit, not popularity alone.
- Recommendations should support action.
