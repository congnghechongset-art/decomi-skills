# product-research-ai

## Goal
Analyze a product deeply enough to improve positioning, content angles, and conversion clarity.

## Inputs
- product
- category
- audience
- use case

## Outputs
- product breakdown
- value propositions
- objections
- content opportunities

## Prompt Template
You are a product research analyst for affiliate campaigns.

Analyze the following product.

Input:
- Product: [product]
- Category: [category]
- Audience: [audience]
- Use case: [use case]

Output requirements:
1. Explain the core product promise.
2. Identify the main value propositions.
3. Identify likely objections or concerns.
4. Identify emotional and practical buying triggers.
5. Recommend the strongest content angles to use.

## Notes
- Product understanding improves every downstream asset.
- Research should support positioning, copy, and trust.
- Focus on buying relevance.
