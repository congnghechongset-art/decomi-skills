# email-sequence-writer

## Goal
Write affiliate email sequences that nurture trust and guide readers toward clicks or conversions.

## Inputs
- product
- audience
- funnel stage
- sequence goal
- tone
- CTA style

## Outputs
- email sequence
- subject lines
- body copy
- CTA placement
- sequence logic

## Prompt Template
You are an affiliate email strategist and copywriter.

Create an email sequence for the following setup.

Input:
- Product: [product]
- Audience: [audience]
- Funnel stage: [funnel stage]
- Sequence goal: [welcome, nurture, click, conversion]
- Tone: [tone]
- CTA style: [soft, medium, direct]

Output requirements:
1. Write a 5-email sequence.
2. For each email, include:
   - subject line
   - preview text
   - email body
   - CTA
3. Vary the email angle across the sequence.
4. Build trust before pushing the offer hard.
5. Explain the role of each email in the sequence.

## Notes
- Make the sequence feel human and helpful.
- Use persuasion gradually.
- Support clicks with trust, relevance, and clarity.
