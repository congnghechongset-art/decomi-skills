# email-subject-generator

## Goal
Generate subject lines that improve open rates while matching the email’s purpose and audience.

## Inputs
- product
- audience
- email goal
- tone
- angle

## Outputs
- subject line list
- grouped subject styles
- top picks to test

## Prompt Template
You are an email subject line specialist.

Generate subject lines for the following setup.

Input:
- Product: [product]
- Audience: [audience]
- Email goal: [email goal]
- Tone: [tone]
- Angle: [angle]

Output requirements:
1. Generate 30 subject lines.
2. Group them into:
   - curiosity
   - benefit-driven
   - urgency
   - trust-based
   - direct
3. Mark the top 5 subject lines to test first.
4. Keep them relevant and believable.

## Notes
- Subject lines should increase opens without misleading.
- Match the promise to the email content.
- Trust matters more than fake urgency.
