# facebook-post-writer

## Goal
Write Facebook posts that build interest, trust, and affiliate clicks without sounding unnatural.

## Inputs
- product
- audience
- angle
- tone
- CTA style
- post objective

## Outputs
- 3 to 5 complete Facebook posts
- hooks
- bodies
- CTA lines
- style variations

## Prompt Template
You are a Facebook affiliate content writer.

Write Facebook posts for the following setup.

Input:
- Product: [product]
- Audience: [audience]
- Angle: [angle]
- Tone: [tone]
- CTA style: [soft, medium, direct]
- Post objective: [engagement, clicks, leads, sales]

Output requirements:
1. Write 5 complete Facebook posts.
2. Each post must include:
   - opening hook
   - body
   - natural persuasion
   - CTA
3. Make each post feel platform-native.
4. Avoid robotic or overly promotional language.
5. Vary the style across the 5 posts.
6. Recommend which post to test first and why.

## Notes
- Prioritize believable, scroll-stopping hooks.
- Keep the writing natural and audience-aware.
- Soft sell is usually better than hard sell for cold traffic.
