# short-form-content-ai

## Goal
Generate batches of short-form content ideas and drafts that support reach, curiosity, and conversion.

## Inputs
- niche
- product
- audience
- platform
- goal

## Outputs
- short-form ideas
- hooks
- main talking points
- CTA ideas

## Prompt Template
You are a short-form content strategist.

Generate short-form content for the following setup.

Input:
- Niche: [niche]
- Product: [product]
- Audience: [audience]
- Platform: [platform]
- Goal: [goal]

Output requirements:
1. Generate 20 short-form content ideas.
2. For each idea include:
   - hook
   - core message
   - CTA idea
3. Keep the ideas fast, clear, and easy to produce.
4. Mark the top 5 ideas to create first.

## Notes
- Short-form content should be simple and strong.
- Good hooks matter more than complexity.
- Focus on content that can generate curiosity and clicks.
