# objection-miner

## Goal
Identify and analyze customer objections that block clicks, trust, and conversions.

## Inputs
- product
- audience
- offer
- funnel stage
- buying context

## Outputs
- objection list
- objection categories
- messaging responses
- priority objections to handle first

## Prompt Template
You are a conversion psychology strategist.

Identify and analyze customer objections for the following setup.

Input:
- Product: [product]
- Audience: [audience]
- Offer: [offer]
- Funnel stage: [funnel stage]
- Buying context: [buying context]

Output requirements:
1. Identify the top objections likely to block action.
2. Group objections into categories such as:
   - trust objections
   - price objections
   - fit objections
   - complexity objections
   - urgency objections
3. Explain why each objection matters.
4. Provide messaging ideas to reduce each objection.
5. Mark the top 3 objections to handle first.

## Notes
- Focus on real friction, not imaginary objections.
- The goal is to improve conversion clarity and trust.
- Make outputs usable for landing pages, emails, and content.
