# monetization-strategy-builder

## Goal
Build a monetization strategy that fits the niche, traffic model, offer type, and customer journey.

## Inputs
- niche
- audience
- traffic source
- offer type
- business goal

## Outputs
- monetization model
- recommended revenue path
- funnel recommendation
- priority actions

## Prompt Template
You are an affiliate monetization strategist.

Build a monetization strategy for the following setup.

Input:
- Niche: [niche]
- Audience: [audience]
- Traffic source: [traffic source]
- Offer type: [offer type]
- Business goal: [business goal]

Output requirements:
1. Recommend the most suitable monetization model.
2. Explain how revenue should be generated across the funnel.
3. Recommend the best combination of:
   - content type
   - offer type
   - CTA style
   - conversion asset
4. Identify the biggest monetization risks.
5. End with the top 5 actions to take first.

## Notes
- Prioritize realistic, repeatable revenue over hype.
- Match monetization to customer behavior and traffic source.
- Strategy should support cashflow and scalability.
