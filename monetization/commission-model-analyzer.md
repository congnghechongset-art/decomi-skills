# commission-model-analyzer

## Goal
Compare commission structures and identify which model offers the best revenue potential for the business context.

## Inputs
- niche
- offer types
- traffic model
- audience quality
- business goal

## Outputs
- commission model comparison
- earning potential analysis
- risk notes
- recommended model

## Prompt Template
You are an affiliate monetization analyst.

Analyze commission models for the following setup.

Input:
- Niche: [niche]
- Offer types: [offer types]
- Traffic model: [traffic model]
- Audience quality: [audience quality]
- Business goal: [business goal]

Output requirements:
1. Compare models such as:
   - CPS
   - CPA
   - recurring
   - hybrid
2. Explain how each model affects revenue potential.
3. Highlight trade-offs and risks.
4. Recommend the best-fit model for this setup.
5. Explain why it should be prioritized.

## Notes
- Highest commission is not always best.
- Match model to trust, audience behavior, and traffic quality.
- Think in terms of real cashflow and scalability.
