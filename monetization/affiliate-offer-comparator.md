# affiliate-offer-comparator

## Goal
Compare multiple affiliate offers and identify which one is best suited for promotion.

## Inputs
- offers
- niche
- audience
- traffic source
- monetization goal

## Outputs
- offer comparison
- strengths and weaknesses
- fit ranking
- recommended offer

## Prompt Template
You are an affiliate offer comparison strategist.

Compare the following offers for this business setup.

Input:
- Offers: [offers]
- Niche: [niche]
- Audience: [audience]
- Traffic source: [traffic source]
- Monetization goal: [monetization goal]

Output requirements:
1. Compare the offers side by side.
2. Evaluate:
   - commission
   - conversion potential
   - trust
   - content fit
   - scalability
3. Rank the offers from best to worst.
4. Recommend the top offer to test first.
5. Explain the commercial reasoning.

## Notes
- Focus on business fit, not brand name alone.
- Best offer should match audience, angle, and channel.
- Ranking should support action.
