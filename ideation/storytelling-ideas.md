# storytelling-ideas

## Goal
Generate story concepts that make affiliate content more human, engaging, and persuasive.

## Inputs
- product
- audience
- platform
- transformation goal
- emotional angle

## Outputs
- story ideas
- emotional triggers
- best formats
- CTA opportunities

## Prompt Template
You are a storytelling strategist for affiliate content.

Generate storytelling ideas for the following setup.

Input:
- Product: [product]
- Audience: [audience]
- Platform: [platform]
- Transformation goal: [transformation goal]
- Emotional angle: [emotional angle]

Output requirements:
1. Generate 12 story concepts.
2. For each concept include:
   - story angle
   - emotional trigger
   - best format
   - CTA opportunity
3. Recommend the top 5 story ideas to test first.

## Notes
- Good story ideas make content feel less promotional.
- Story should connect with audience pain or desire.
- Keep ideas commercially useful.
