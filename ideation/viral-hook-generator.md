# viral-hook-generator

## Goal
Generate strong hooks that stop attention and increase the chance of clicks, views, or engagement.

## Inputs
- niche
- product
- audience
- platform
- pain point
- hook style

## Outputs
- hook list
- grouped hook categories
- recommended hooks to test first

## Prompt Template
You are a viral hook strategist for affiliate marketing.

Generate high-performing hooks for the following setup.

Input:
- Niche: [niche]
- Product: [product]
- Audience: [audience]
- Platform: [platform]
- Pain point: [pain point]
- Hook style: [curiosity, pain, authority, transformation, contrarian]

Output requirements:
1. Generate 30 hooks.
2. Group them into:
   - curiosity hooks
   - pain-point hooks
   - authority hooks
   - transformation hooks
   - contrarian hooks
3. Mark the top 5 hooks to test first.
4. Keep the hooks short, platform-native, and commercially relevant.

## Notes
- Prioritize hooks that create immediate curiosity or emotional relevance.
- Avoid vague hooks with no clear angle.
- Hooks should support traffic and conversion, not just vanity engagement.
