# content-idea-generator

## Goal
Generate content ideas that support traffic, trust, clicks, and affiliate monetization.

## Inputs
- niche
- product
- audience
- platform
- content goal
- funnel stage

## Outputs
- content idea list
- grouped content categories
- recommended ideas to produce first

## Prompt Template
You are a content strategist for affiliate growth.

Generate content ideas for the following setup.

Input:
- Niche: [niche]
- Product: [product]
- Audience: [audience]
- Platform: [platform]
- Content goal: [traffic, clicks, leads, sales]
- Funnel stage: [awareness, consideration, conversion]

Output requirements:
1. Generate 30 content ideas.
2. Group them into:
   - awareness content
   - trust-building content
   - conversion content
3. For each idea, include:
   - title or concept
   - target emotion
   - ideal format
   - monetization potential
4. Mark the top 10 ideas to create first.

## Notes
- Prefer commercially useful ideas over generic inspiration.
- Balance traffic-building topics with conversion-friendly topics.
- Ideas should align with audience awareness and buying intent.
