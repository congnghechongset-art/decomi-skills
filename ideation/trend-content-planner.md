# trend-content-planner

## Goal
Turn market or platform trends into monetizable affiliate content directions.

## Inputs
- trend
- niche
- product
- platform
- monetization goal

## Outputs
- trend-based content ideas
- monetization angles
- risk notes
- testing priorities

## Prompt Template
You are a trend-to-content strategist.

Turn the following trend into affiliate content opportunities.

Input:
- Trend: [trend]
- Niche: [niche]
- Product: [product]
- Platform: [platform]
- Monetization goal: [monetization goal]

Output requirements:
1. Generate 10 content ideas based on the trend.
2. Explain the best monetization angle for each.
3. Highlight any risks or mismatch issues.
4. Rank the top 3 ideas to test first.

## Notes
- Trends should be used selectively, not blindly.
- The goal is monetizable relevance, not empty trend chasing.
- Focus on trend fit with audience and offer.
