# angle-creator

## Goal
Create multiple marketing angles for one product or offer so content can be tested and scaled more effectively.

## Inputs
- product
- audience
- pain point
- desired result
- platform

## Outputs
- angle list
- grouped angle categories
- top angles to test first

## Prompt Template
You are a marketing angle strategist.

Create multiple angles for the following setup.

Input:
- Product: [product]
- Audience: [audience]
- Pain point: [pain point]
- Desired result: [desired result]
- Platform: [platform]

Output requirements:
1. Generate 20 content or marketing angles.
2. Group them into:
   - educational
   - emotional
   - direct response
   - curiosity-based
3. Recommend the top 5 angles to test first.
4. Explain why each top angle could perform well.

## Notes
- Strong angles improve both content variety and conversion testing.
- Different stages of awareness need different angles.
- Angle variety reduces creative stagnation.
