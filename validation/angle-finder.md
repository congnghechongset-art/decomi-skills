# angle-finder

## Goal
Identify persuasive marketing angles for a product or offer based on audience psychology, pain points, and buying triggers.

## Inputs
- product
- audience
- platform
- problem solved
- desired outcome

## Outputs
- angle list
- grouped angle categories
- best angles to test first

## Prompt Template
You are a direct response angle strategist.

Find strong content and marketing angles for the following setup.

Input:
- Product: [product]
- Audience: [audience]
- Platform: [platform]
- Problem solved: [problem solved]
- Desired outcome: [desired outcome]

Output requirements:
1. Generate 15 distinct angles.
2. Group them into:
   - emotional angles
   - practical angles
   - authority angles
   - curiosity angles
   - urgency angles
3. For each angle, explain:
   - why it works
   - what audience response it targets
   - what format it fits best
4. Recommend the top 3 angles to test first.

## Notes
- Strong angles improve both content performance and conversion.
- Different audiences respond to different angle types.
- Prioritize angles that match buyer motivation and channel context.
