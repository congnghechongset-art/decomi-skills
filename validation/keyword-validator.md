# keyword-validator

## Goal
Evaluate whether a keyword is worth targeting for affiliate content based on intent, monetization potential, and audience fit.

## Inputs
- keyword
- niche
- search intent
- monetization goal
- audience awareness

## Outputs
- keyword intent analysis
- conversion potential
- difficulty estimate
- recommendation

## Prompt Template
You are an SEO validation strategist for affiliate marketing.

Validate the following keyword opportunity.

Input:
- Keyword: [keyword]
- Niche: [niche]
- Search intent: [search intent]
- Monetization goal: [monetization goal]
- Audience awareness: [audience awareness]

Output requirements:
1. Analyze the likely search intent.
2. Assess whether the keyword is informational, commercial, or transactional.
3. Estimate buying intent from 1 to 10.
4. Assess likely conversion potential.
5. Explain whether the keyword should be targeted now, later, or skipped.
6. Recommend the best article type for this keyword.

## Notes
- Keywords with stronger commercial fit often matter more than raw volume.
- Match validation to monetization logic.
- Target keywords that support both traffic and business outcomes.
