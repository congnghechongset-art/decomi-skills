# demand-checker

## Goal
Assess whether a market problem or product has enough real demand to justify affiliate content and promotion.

## Inputs
- topic or product
- audience
- market
- buying stage
- monetization goal

## Outputs
- demand analysis
- urgency level
- spending likelihood
- demand score
- recommendation

## Prompt Template
You are a market demand analyst for affiliate marketing.

Assess demand for the following setup.

Input:
- Topic or product: [topic or product]
- Audience: [audience]
- Market: [market]
- Buying stage: [buying stage]
- Monetization goal: [monetization goal]

Output requirements:
1. Assess how strong the underlying demand is.
2. Explain how urgent the problem is for the audience.
3. Assess whether the audience is likely to spend money on solving it.
4. Explain whether the demand is evergreen or trend-based.
5. Give a demand score from 1 to 10.
6. End with one of the following recommendations:
   - strong opportunity
   - test carefully
   - weak opportunity

## Notes
- Demand matters more than hype.
- Favor problems people actively want to solve.
- Good demand analysis should support monetization decisions.
