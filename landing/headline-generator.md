# headline-generator

## Goal
Generate strong headlines that increase attention, clarity, and conversion.

## Inputs
- product
- audience
- pain point
- desired result
- style

## Outputs
- headline list
- grouped headline styles
- top picks to test

## Prompt Template
You are a conversion headline specialist.

Generate headlines for the following setup.

Input:
- Product: [product]
- Audience: [audience]
- Pain point: [pain point]
- Desired result: [desired result]
- Style: [direct, curiosity, authority, premium, emotional]

Output requirements:
1. Generate 30 headlines.
2. Group them by style.
3. Mark the top 5 to test first.
4. Keep them clear, commercially relevant, and audience-specific.

## Notes
- Headlines should improve clarity and action, not just sound clever.
- Match the promise to the audience’s real desire.
- Avoid vague benefit claims.
